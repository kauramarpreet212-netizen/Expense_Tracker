Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect information you provide directly — including your name, email address, and password when you register. We also collect the expense data you enter, such as amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We may additionally collect basic usage data (e.g. pages visited, actions taken) to help us improve the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expenses, generating summaries and breakdowns, and communicating important updates about your account. We do not use your financial data for advertising purposes, and we never sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you choose to delete your account, your data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days. While we take reasonable precautions to protect your data, no system is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to operate — such as hosting providers and analytics tools. These services may have access to limited data necessary to perform their functions, and are contractually obligated not to use it for other purposes. We do not integrate with any advertising networks or data brokers.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you at any time. You may also request a copy of your data in a portable format. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below. We will respond to all requests within 30 days.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please reach out to us at support@spendly.app.
